Our Maison

Born of optics, raised by art

"A frame should disappear on the face and reappear in the mirror."

LUMIÈRE began in 2008 in a small Florence workshop, founded by optical engineer Sofia Venturi and designer Henri Laurent. Their idea was simple but stubborn: never compromise fit for fashion, or fashion for fit.

  • 42-step hand finishing on every acetate frame
  • Lab-verified lenses to ±0.02 dioptre
  • Carbon-neutral shipping since 2021
  • Lifetime adjustment & repair guarantee

The Craft

From a block of acetate to a second skin

i

Select

Italian Mazzucchelli acetate, cured for months to deepen colour and resist warping.

ii

Cut

CNC-milled fronts and temples, then hand-tumbled for 36 hours to a silk finish.

iii

Fit

3D facial mapping sets temple splay, pantoscopic tilt and vertex distance per wearer.

iv

Finish

Hand-polished, hinge-set, and paired with lenses edged to 0.1 mm in our lab.

Sustainability

Eyewear that costs the planet less

Our cases are molded from recycled ocean-bound leather offcuts. Frames are repairable for life, and we recover and recycle 94% of workshop offcuts into new acetate stock. Every pair ships plastic-free.
